About Us

Studycomb was designed by students for students. As students ourselves, we know that integrating accountability, social interaction, time management, and physical & mental well being into our student life is essential to student success. With the COVID-19 pandemic preventing in-person classes, it has been more difficult than ever for students to include these practices in their study habits. Studycomb combines these 4 core values into a web application that students can use to improve their studying habits and supplement online school.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join and become a bee?

Any student can become a student bee! Studycomb is primarily created for university students and students completing school online but any student can join as long as they pass our verification process!

Why is a verification process necessary?

Due to the nature of video calls, we want to protect the privacy of all our student bees by ensuring that all student bees are real students who want to learn. This process ensures we can create a safe, and welcoming learning environment for all our bees.

How will study rooms, social events, and workshops run?

When you make an account, you will gain access to your own personal student nest where you can find the links to each of these events. These events will be run primarily through Jitzi.

How much does it cost?

Nothing! We know how much it costs to be a student; we created Studycomb with equity and accessibility in mind which is why we rely on student bee participation to help run our study rooms, social rooms, and workshops.

Independent Study room VS Group Study room?

In independent study rooms, the host will be leading student bees through common quiet studying techniques like the Pomodoro Method. In group study rooms, student bees can sign up to host rooms where they can teach material to other bees which will help solidify their understanding of the topic through the Prot√©g√© Effect. Fellow student bees can attend these teaching rooms to learn a new topic or review a topic they have already learned.

How do I join?

Click the Login/Register bottom at the top right of our screen to get started with our verification process! Make sure to use your school email so we can verify your identity!
